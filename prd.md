# 1. Project Overview

Adah Radah Company is a Nigerian business consulting and registration company that helps entrepreneurs, startups, SMEs, NGOs, and organizations register and manage their businesses with relevant government agencies.

The goal of this project is to build a modern, professional, and conversion-focused website that establishes trust, showcases the company's services, and enables visitors to easily inquire about or begin the registration process.

The website should position Adah Radah Company as a reliable partner for business registration and compliance services in Nigeria.
